Login Help

How to Login:

  1. Select your role from the dropdown
  2. Enter your Staff ID
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click Login to access your dashboard

Available Roles:

  • Doctor: Access patient records, prescriptions, and medical reports
  • Pharmacist: Manage medicine inventory and prescriptions
  • Receptionist: Handle patient registration and appointments
  • Lab Technician: Manage laboratory tests and reports

Contact Support:

If you're having trouble logging in, contact your system administrator.
